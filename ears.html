Ears

  • Causative pathogens
    • M. catarrhalis, S. pneumoniae, H. Influenzae
    • 1st line therapy
      • if no ABX in last 30 days: amoxicillin 80-90 mg/kg/day BID x 5-10 days
    • 2nd line therapy
      • if received ABX in last 30 days: amoxicillin-cluvulanate 90 mg/kg/day BID x 5-10 days
      • amoxicillin with cluvulanate 6.4 mg/kg/day BID x 5-10 days
      • Cefdinir 14 mg/kg/day BID x 5-10 days
      • Cefpodoxime proxetil 10 mg/kg/day BID x 5-10 days
      • Cefprozil 15 mg/kg/day BID x 5-10 days
      • Cefuroxime axetil 30 mg/kg/day BID x 5-10 days
    • Alternative therapies
      • skin rash allergy to PCNs: omnicef 14 mg/kg BID/daily x 5-10 days per age
      • severe allergy to PCNs: azithromycin 10 mg/kg on day 1, decrease 5 mg/kg on days 2-5
      • PO treatment noncompliance/received treatent within last 30 days: ceftriaxone IM 50 mg/kg x 1-3 days

  • Causative pathogens
    • S. aureas, P. aeruginosa
    • fungal: candida sp.
    • 1st line therapy
      • ciprofloxin (+ dexamethasone/hydrocortisone) otic drops: 3 drops/infected ear BID x 7 days
    • 2nd line therapy
      • neomycin/polymyxin B: 3 drops/infected ear TID/QID x 10 days
    • fungal:
      • fluconazole 200 mg x1 dose, then 100 mg x 3-5 days
