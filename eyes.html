Eyes

  • Causative pathogens
    • S. aureas, S. pneumoniae, M. cararrhalis, H. Influenzae, Viridans Strep, Moraxella sp.
    • 1st line therapy
      • polymyxin B/trimethoprim sulfate opthalmic eye drops: 4-6 drops/day x 7 days
    • 2nd line therapy
      • levofloxacin 0.5% 1-2 drops Q2-4 hrs x 7 days
    • Alternative therapies
      • GC-CT infections: erythromycin 0.5% opthalmic ointment QID x 14 days

  • Causative pathogens
    • S. aureas
    • 1st line therapy
      • erythromycin 0.5% opthalmic ointment QID x 14 days
      • apply warm compresses and gentle massages
    • 2nd line therapy
      • polytrim 2 drops 4-6x/day x 7-10 days

  • Causative pathogens
    • C. trachomatis, N. gonorrhoeae, HSV (viral)
    • 1st line therapy
      • C. trachomatis: oral erythromycin 50 mg/kg/day TID x 14 days
      • N. gonorrhoeae: ceftriaxone 25-50 mg/kg/day IV/IM; should not exceed more than 125 mg
      • HSV: acyclovir IV 20mg/kg/dose BID/TID (based on age of neonate) x 2 weeks
    • 2nd line therapy
      • hyperbillibinemia: cefotaxime 25 mg/kg BID
    • Alternative therapies
      • prophylaxis: erythromycin 0.5% optic ointment at birth
